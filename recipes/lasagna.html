Lasagna

Description

Here are the steps to make some good lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to tast
  • 1 (16 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

<
  1. Step 1: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes until al dente. Then drain.
  2. Step 2: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In a 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese. Mix until pan is filled.
  4. Step 4: Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese in melted and bubbly.
